Starlight Garden Rules Please follow

Main rules

  • Cursing and swearing are not allowed on this public server
  • Please build houses away from our spawn
  • You must build your house at least 456789 blocks away from spawn. Or ask the owner/admin for a sky island to build
  • Carbon Fiber Blocks, Steel Blocks, Iron Blocks, Titanium Blocks are restricted for houses. If you love those blocks, please wait for us to supply you a freebuild area for you to build
  • Asking for admin and moderator are illegal. Please remember that you'll earn them. Asking also decrease your chance by 70%. So please remember
  • If you had found a gem tree, tell an admin to protect it. Do not break it!
  • Please if you had found any cave trolls, do not kill them. Also make them a home and tell an admin to protect.
  • Stealing can be accepted as a raid, but do not steal when admins are here
  • No inappropriate languages or bad words are allowed in this server.
  • If you break one of our rules, we will warn you 1 time as a kick, 3 kicks will result as a ban
  • Please respect admins and mods as a work
  • Advertising other server in our server is illegal and restricted. We will punish you as a ban
  • Being rude are not allowed

Rules for moderator(mods)

  • Do not ban/kick people for no reason, it will result as an unmod or a ban
  • Follow the main rules too
  • Respect admin and the handsome owner as your work

Rules for administrator(admins)

  • Respect the handsome owner
  • Banning or kicking other people for no reason or just for fun will result as an unadmin and maybe 20% of a free ban
  • Do not admin people without the owner’s permission
